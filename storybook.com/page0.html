About Iggy

Iggy is a smart kid who is crazy about architecture. His parents are proud of his passion and his work ethic, although at the same time they are amazed by his skills. Iggy had it going easy until he met his teacher. The story is journey about Iggy, his skills, his adventures and how his hardwork yielded great results for him.
